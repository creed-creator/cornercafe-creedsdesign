Our Story

A Little Spot with
Big Heart

Corner Cafe has been a Decatur staple for homemade breakfast and lunch. Every dish is made from scratch — the soups, the pies, the sauces. Nothing comes out of a bag here.

Tucked on 13th Street, we're the kind of place where the owner knows your name and the food tastes like someone's kitchen — because it does.
