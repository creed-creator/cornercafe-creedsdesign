Our Story

Homemade food, a warm welcome

Corner Cafe has been a staple of downtown Decatur since 2016. Everything on the menu is made from scratch — from the hand-breaded tenderloins to the homemade pies cooling on the counter. We're a small place with big flavor, and we're proud of every plate we put out.
